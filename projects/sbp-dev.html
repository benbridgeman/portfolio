SBP Development using Joomla

This was a tough one, my first experience of reverse-engineering another dev's work. The previous dev built the site using Joomla, however a lot of the site was not functioning as intended, also after a few years it had lost its edge aesthetically. I spent a lot of time looking through and inevitably teaching myself php, and managed to fix the site's functionality, allowing admins to create site-wide articles that appeared in the correct places rather than recreating those articles multiple times in each of those places.
