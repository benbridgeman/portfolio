D&AD Entry - Little White Lies Magazine

The task was to create an illustrated cover for Little White Lies magazine (a movie enthusiast magazine featuring old and new), I had to pick one of 4 films to be the subject of the cover, Tinker Tailor Soldier Spy stood out to me as 'my-kind-of-film'. I started with a portrait of Gary Oldman as George Smiley and began creating the composition with vectors in Adobe Illustrator for a 'painting-by-numbers' style cover which I thought to be fitting of the film's theme (filling in the blanks to solve the puzzle), below is the result.
